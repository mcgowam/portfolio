Joe Smiths

I am a recent graduate with a Bachelor's degree in 3D modeling and design, and I am excited to start my career as a 3D modeler for video games. During my studies, I developed a strong foundation in 3D modeling software such as Maya, ZBrush, and 3ds Max, and I have experience working on projects in both individual and team settings. I am passionate about video games and fascinated by the process of bringing game worlds to life through 3D modeling and design.

Aside from 3D modeling, I also have experience in graphic design, and I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. My background in graphic design has given me an eye for detail and composition, which I apply to my 3D modeling work to ensure that the models are both visually appealing and functional for their intended use in the game.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, watching movies, and drawing. These hobbies have helped me develop my creativity and attention to detail, which I bring to my 3D modeling work. I am a quick learner and eager to continue developing my skills in 3D modeling and design, and I am excited to contribute my passion and enthusiasm to a team that is committed to creating amazing video game experiences."
